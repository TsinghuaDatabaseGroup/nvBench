  1. A pie chart for showing the number of the dates of performances with attending members whose roles are "Violin".

  2. Show the number of the dates of performances with attending members whose roles are "Violin".

  3. Show the dates of performances with attending members whose roles are "Violin", and count them by a pie chart

VIS Query:

Visualize PIE SELECT Date , COUNT(Date) FROM member_attendance AS T1 JOIN member AS T2 ON T1.Member_ID = T2.Member_ID JOIN performance AS T3 ON T1.Performance_ID = T3.Performance_ID WHERE T2.Role = "Violin" GROUP BY Date
