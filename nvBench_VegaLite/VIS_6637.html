  1. A bar chart for what are the number of the names of the technicians that are assigned to repair machines with more point values than 70?, and could you sort how many name from low to high order?

VIS Query:

Visualize BAR SELECT Name , COUNT(Name) FROM repair_assignment AS T1 JOIN machine AS T2 ON T1.machine_id = T2.machine_id JOIN technician AS T3 ON T1.technician_ID = T3.technician_ID WHERE T2.value_points > 70 GROUP BY Name ORDER BY COUNT(Name) ASC
