NL Queries:

  1. What are the number of the names of the technicians that are assigned to repair machines with more point values than 70?, and order from high to low by the total number.

  2. A bar chart for what are the number of the names of the technicians that are assigned to repair machines with more point values than 70?, and sort total number from high to low order please.

  3. What are the names of the technicians that are assigned to repair machines with more point values than 70, and count them by a bar chart, and could you sort Y-axis in descending order?

  4. Show names of technicians who are assigned to repair machines with value point more than 70, and count them by a bar chart, order by the y-axis in desc.

VIS Query:

Visualize BAR SELECT Name , COUNT(Name) FROM repair_assignment AS T1 JOIN machine AS T2 ON T1.machine_id = T2.machine_id JOIN technician AS T3 ON T1.technician_ID = T3.technician_ID WHERE T2.value_points > 70 GROUP BY Name ORDER BY COUNT(Name) DESC
