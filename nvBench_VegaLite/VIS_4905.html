NL Queries:

  1. List the number of dogs of the rarest breed in each treatment day with group a line chart that bins date of treatment by day and groups by dog's name.

  2. How many dogs of the rarest breed in each treatment day? Draw a group line chart that bins date of treatment by day and groups by dog's name.

VIS Query:

Visualize LINE SELECT date_of_treatment , COUNT(date_of_treatment) FROM Dogs AS T1 JOIN Treatments AS T2 ON T1.dog_id = T2.dog_id WHERE T1.breed_code = ( SELECT breed_code FROM Dogs GROUP BY breed_code ORDER BY count(*) ASC LIMIT 1 ) name BIN date_of_treatment BY DAY
