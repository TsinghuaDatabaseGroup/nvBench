NL Queries:

  1. For all players, show the number of different club names they come from, and display y axis in desc order.

  2. For the names and the players of clubs, count the names by names in a bar chart, and rank by the y-axis from high to low.

VIS Query:

Visualize BAR SELECT T1.name , COUNT(T1.name) FROM club AS T1 JOIN player AS T2 ON T1.Club_ID = T2.Club_ID GROUP BY T1.name ORDER BY COUNT(T1.name) DESC
