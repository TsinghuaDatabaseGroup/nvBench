  1. What are the number of the names of the singers who performed in a concert in 2014?, order by the the number of name from low to high.

  2. List all singer names in concerts in year 2014, and count them by a bar chart, and show by the Y-axis from low to high.

  3. A bar chart for what are the number of the names of the singers who performed in a concert in 2014?, order by the the number of name from low to high.

  4. What are the names of the singers who performed in a concert in 2014, and count them by a bar chart, and list the number of name from low to high order.

VIS Query:

Visualize BAR SELECT Name , COUNT(Name) FROM singer_in_concert AS T1 JOIN singer AS T2 ON T1.singer_id = T2.singer_id JOIN concert AS T3 ON T1.concert_id = T3.concert_id WHERE T3.year = 2014 GROUP BY Name ORDER BY COUNT(Name) ASC
